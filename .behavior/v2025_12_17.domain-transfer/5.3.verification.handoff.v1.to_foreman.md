## what makes this impossible without foreman intervention

integration tests require:
- `SQUARESPACE_EMAIL` — foreman's squarespace account email
- `SQUARESPACE_PASSWORD` — foreman's squarespace account password
- `SQUARESPACE_TOTP_SECRET` (optional) — foreman's TOTP secret if 2FA enabled

these are real credentials to access a real squarespace account. only the foreman possesses them.

---

## is this truly a "foreman possesses the key" situation?

**yes.** the credentials are:
- account-specific authentication secrets
- not safe to store in environment or repo
- required for the tests to verify real playwright automation
