# Squarespace Domain Management - Domain Research

## Overview

This research documents the domain objects and operations available for managing Squarespace domains, specifically focused on:
- Enumerating domains in Squarespace
- Viewing DNS records
- Transferring domains out of Squarespace to another registrar (e.g., Cloudflare)

Since Squarespace does not provide public APIs for domain management, automation will require Playwright-based UI interactions.

---

## Domain Objects

### Entities

#### 1. SquarespaceDomain

The primary entity representing a domain registered with or managed by Squarespace.

**Properties:**
- `name` - The domain name (e.g., "example.com")
- `status` - Domain status (Active, Expired, Pending)
- `isLocked` - Whether domain transfer lock is enabled
- `registrar` - The registrar of record [1]
  - "Squarespace Domains LLC"
  - "Squarespace Domains II LLC"
  - "Tucows" (legacy)
  - "Key-Systems" (for certain TLDs)
- `expirationDate` - When the domain registration expires
- `autoRenew` - Whether auto-renewal is enabled
- `createdAt` - Domain registration date
- `dnssecEnabled` - Whether DNSSEC is enabled

> "Squarespace has historically used two registrars to manage domains—Squarespace Domains LLC and Tucows—and recently purchased a portfolio of domains from Google Domains." [1]

#### 2. SquarespaceDomainContact

Contact information associated with a domain registration.

**Properties:**
- `type` - Contact type [2]
  - `Registrant` - "The primary contact for the domain, and the only contact who receives domain-related emails."
  - `Admin` - "Serves as a secondary contact but doesn't receive domain emails"
  - `Tech` - "Supports the functionality of the domain"
  - `Billing` - "Not publicly displayed; never appears in registrar lookups"
- `name` - Full name
- `organization` - Organization name (determines legal ownership if populated) [2]
- `email` - Email address
- `phone` - Phone number (no dashes, no country code initially) [2]
- `address` - Complete address with postal code
- `country` - Country

> "For gTLD Registrant email changes, verify via email confirmation" [2]

#### 3. SquarespaceDnsRecord

A DNS record associated with a domain.

**Properties:**
- `type` - Record type [3][4]
  - Web Hosting: `A`, `AAAA`, `ALIAS`, `CNAME`
  - Email: `DKIM`, `SPF`, `DMARC`, `MX`, `SRV`
  - Security: `CAA`, `DS`, `DNSKEY`, `HTTPS`, `PTR`, `SSHFP`, `TLSA`
  - Other: `NS`, `TXT`
- `host` - The host/subdomain (e.g., "@" for root, "www", "mail")
- `value` - The record value (IP address, hostname, text content, etc.)
- `ttl` - Time to Live [4]
  - Default: 4 hours (14400 seconds)
  - Maximum: 2,147,483,648 seconds
- `priority` - Priority value (for MX, SRV records)

> "Custom records have a 4-hour TTL by default, but you can change that if you have specific needs." [4]

#### 4. SquarespaceDomainForwarding

Domain forwarding configuration.

**Properties:**
- `sourceDomain` - The domain being forwarded
- `destinationUrl` - Where the domain redirects to
- `redirectType` - 301 (permanent) or 302 (temporary)

#### 5. SquarespaceEmailForwarding

Email forwarding configuration.

**Properties:**
- `alias` - The email alias (e.g., "info@example.com")
- `destinationEmail` - Where emails are forwarded to

> "Free domain and email forwarding (when using appropriate nameservers)" [5]

#### 6. SquarespaceDomainTransfer

Represents a domain transfer request.

**Properties:**
- `domain` - The domain being transferred
- `authorizationCode` - EPP/transfer code
- `status` - Transfer status
- `initiatedAt` - When transfer was initiated
- `completedAt` - When transfer completed (if applicable)

---

### Events

#### 1. DomainTransferInitiated
Triggered when a transfer code is requested.

> "Email arrives from customercare@squarespace.com with subject 'Auth code for [yourdomain.com]'" [1]

#### 2. DomainTransferCodeReceived
Triggered when authorization code email is received.

> "Code should arrive within 24 hours" [1]

#### 3. DomainTransferGracePeriodStarted
For Squarespace Domains LLC registrar.

> "If your domain's registrar is Squarespace Domains LLC or Squarespace Domains II LLC, after you complete the steps above, you'll receive an email from us confirming that your transfer is in progress. You'll have up to 5 days to reject your domain transfer via this email." [1]

#### 4. DomainTransferCompleted
Triggered when transfer finishes.

> "The transfer process can take up to 15 days to complete" [1]

#### 5. DomainUnlocked
Triggered when domain lock is toggled off.

#### 6. DnsRecordChanged
Triggered when a DNS record is added, modified, or deleted.

## Domain Operations

### Get Operations

#### getAllDomains
**Purpose:** Enumerate all domains in the Squarespace account
**UI Location:** `account.squarespace.com/domains` [3]
**Returns:** List of `SquarespaceDomain` entities

#### getOneDomain
**Purpose:** Get details for a specific domain
**UI Location:** `account.squarespace.com/domains` → click domain name
**Returns:** Single `SquarespaceDomain` with full details

#### getDomainDnsRecords
**Purpose:** Get all DNS records for a domain
**UI Location:** Domain → DNS → DNS Settings [3][4]
**Returns:** List of `SquarespaceDnsRecord` entities

> "Open your domains dashboard, click the domain name, and click DNS in the side panel." [3]

#### getDomainContacts
**Purpose:** Get registration contact information
**UI Location:** Domain → Registration information → Edit [2]
**Returns:** List of `SquarespaceDomainContact` entities (Registrant, Admin, Tech, Billing)

#### getDomainLockStatus
**Purpose:** Check if domain is locked for transfer
**UI Location:** Domain details page
**Returns:** Boolean + lock reason if locked

#### getDomainForwarding
**Purpose:** Get domain forwarding configuration
**UI Location:** Domain → Forwarding settings
**Returns:** `SquarespaceDomainForwarding` or null

#### getEmailForwarding
**Purpose:** Get email forwarding rules
**UI Location:** Domain → Email settings
**Returns:** List of `SquarespaceEmailForwarding` entities

---

### Set Operations

#### setDomainUnlock
**Purpose:** Unlock a domain for transfer
**UI Location:** Domain details → toggle Domain Lock off
**Prerequisites:**
- Domain not in 60-day registration lock [1]
- Domain not in 60-day transfer lock [1]
- No pending contact changes [1]

> "Toggle off the Domain Lock" [1]

#### setRequestTransferCode
**Purpose:** Request authorization/EPP code for transfer
**UI Location:** Domain details → "Request transfer code" button
**Authentication:** Requires re-authentication [1]
**Result:** Email sent within 24 hours [1]

> "Click 'Request transfer code' and reauthenticate" [1]
> "Multiple code requests generate multiple unique codes—only the most recent remains valid" [1]

#### setDnsRecord (Create)
**Purpose:** Add a new DNS record
**UI Location:** Domain → DNS → DNS Settings → Custom Records → Add record [4]
**Authentication:** Requires password or 2FA re-authentication [4]
**Constraints:**
- "It's not possible to add MX records for multiple different email services, or multiple CNAME records with the same host value" [4]

> "Click Add record. Enter your current password, or if you have two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled, reauthenticate your account using your 2FA method and click Continue." [4]

#### setDnsRecord (Update)
**Purpose:** Modify an existing DNS record
**UI Location:** Domain → DNS → DNS Settings → Custom Records
**Note:** May require delete + create for certain record types

#### delDnsRecord
**Purpose:** Delete a DNS record
**UI Location:** Domain → DNS → Custom Records → hover → trash icon [4]

> "Scrolling to Custom Records, hovering over the target record, clicking the trash icon, and reauthenticating if necessary." [4]

#### setDomainContact
**Purpose:** Update registration contact information
**UI Location:** Domain → Registration information → Edit → Save [2]
**Constraints:**
- "No special characters like ü, é, ñ" [2]
- Email changes require verification [2]

#### setDnssec (Enable/Disable)
**Purpose:** Toggle DNSSEC for domain
**UI Location:** Domain → Advanced settings
**Note:** "Remove DNSSEC from advanced settings if previously added" before transfer [1]

---

### Delete Operations

#### delDomainForwarding
**Purpose:** Remove domain forwarding
**UI Location:** Domain → Forwarding settings

#### delEmailForwarding
**Purpose:** Remove email forwarding rule
**UI Location:** Domain → Email settings

## UI Navigation Structure

Based on documentation, the Squarespace Domains UI is structured as:

```
account.squarespace.com/domains
├── [Domain List View]
│   └── Each domain row shows: name, status, expiration
│
└── [Domain Detail View] (click domain name)
    ├── Overview
    │   ├── Domain Lock toggle
    │   ├── Request transfer code button
    │   └── Auto-renew settings
    │
    ├── DNS (side panel)
    │   ├── Add Preset button (top-right)
    │   ├── DNS Settings section
    │   │   └── Preset records with trash icons
    │   └── Custom Records section
    │       └── Add record button
    │
    ├── Registration information
    │   ├── Edit button
    │   └── Contact forms (Registrant, Admin, Tech, Billing)
    │
    └── Advanced Settings
        └── DNSSEC toggle
```

## Transfer Process Flow

The documented transfer-out process [1]:

1. **Access dashboard:** `account.squarespace.com/domains`
2. **Select domain:** Click domain to transfer
3. **Disable DNSSEC:** Remove from advanced settings if enabled
4. **Screenshot DNS:** Document current records for reference
5. **Unlock domain:** Toggle Domain Lock off
6. **Request code:** Click "Request transfer code" → reauthenticate
7. **Wait for email:** Up to 24 hours, from `customercare@squarespace.com`
8. **Copy code:** Get authorization code from email
9. **Initiate at new registrar:** Provide code to Cloudflare/destination
10. **Wait for transfer:** Up to 15 days to complete
11. **Grace period:** 5 days to cancel (Squarespace Domains LLC) [1]

> "After transfer completes, the domain appears as a third-party domain in your dashboard, and DNS edits occur through the new provider only." [1]

## Constraints and Limitations

### Transfer Restrictions [1]
- 60-day lock after registration
- 60-day lock after inbound transfer
- Lock after registrant contact changes
- Expired domains: 30-day window to transfer

### DNS Restrictions [4]
- Cannot have multiple MX records for different email services
- Cannot have multiple CNAME records with same host
- TTL propagation: 24-48 hours
- Nameserver Connect limits records to: A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, SRV, TXT

### Unsupported Features [5]
- Dynamic DNS (DDNS)
- ACME TLS/SSL certificates (new issuance)
- Squarespace 5 site connections
