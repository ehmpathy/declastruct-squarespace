## why might these have been added?

### hypothesis 1: "while we're here"

the blueprint pattern from research (prior/3.1.research.domain._.v1.i1.md) included dns crud operations as part of a generic declastruct template. this was likely carried over without validating against the wish.

### hypothesis 2: "future flexibility"

dns management might be useful someday for domains that remain in squarespace. but this is speculative — the wish is specifically about **transfer out**, not manage in place.

### hypothesis 3: "symmetry with domain registration"

domain registration has set operations (unlock, disable dnssec). dns might have been given set/del for symmetry. but this symmetry wasn't requested.
