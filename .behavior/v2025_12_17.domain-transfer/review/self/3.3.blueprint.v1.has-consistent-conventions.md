## divergence check

### divergence from prior research

compared implementation against prior research artifacts:
- `prior/3.1.research.domain._.v1.i1.md` — initial domain research
- `prior/3.2.distill.domain._.v1.i1.md` — distilled design

| prior research | prior distillation | implementation | divergent? | justified? |
|----------------|-------------------|----------------|------------|------------|
| `SquarespaceDomain` | `DeclaredSquarespaceDomain` | `DeclaredSquarespaceDomainRegistration` | yes | yes |
| `SquarespaceDnsRecord` | `DeclaredSquarespaceDnsRecord` | `DeclaredSquarespaceDomainDnsRecord` | yes | yes |
| `SquarespaceDomainTransfer` | `DeclaredSquarespaceDomainTransferRequest` | `DeclaredSquarespaceDomainTransferRequest` | no | — |

**divergence analysis:**

1. **`DeclaredSquarespaceDomainRegistration`** vs `DeclaredSquarespaceDomain`
   - why diverged: `Domain` is ambiguous (could mean dns zone, domain object, etc.)
   - why justified: `Registration` clarifies this is the registrar registration entity
   - aligns with: domain term precision (rule.require.ubiqlang)

2. **`DeclaredSquarespaceDomainDnsRecord`** vs `DeclaredSquarespaceDnsRecord`
   - why diverged: adds `Domain` as parent entity prefix
   - why justified: follows pattern `{Parent}{Child}` for child entities
   - aligns with: hierarchical entity names

**verdict: divergence is intentional and justified.** names are more precise than prior research.
